Automatic Advanced Matching (email field)

The identity plugin (loaded via signals-config.js) scans the entire page for PII fields and hashes them automatically. Type an email, click Submit, and ud[em] will appear in the beacon. Once captured, the hash persists for the page session and attaches to all subsequent beacons from any event, including InferredEvents.
